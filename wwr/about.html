History

Founded in 1998 by two former Olympic kayakers, Elias and Sofia, our rafting company started as a small, seasonal operation with just two borrowed rafts and a lot of passion for the river. Our mission was simple: make the thrilling experience of whitewater accessible to everyone** while teaching stewardship of the natural environment.

Over the last two decades, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted adventure outfits. We now run year-round expeditions and have expanded our fleet, but our dedication remains the same: prioritizing safety, professionalism, and unforgettable adventure**. We continue to work closely with local conservation groups to ensure the rivers we love remain pristine for generations of rafters to come.
